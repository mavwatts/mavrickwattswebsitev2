Luciana's Mexican Resteraunt and Cantina

Luciana's Mexican Resteraunt and Cantina is a mexican resteraunt with locations in Indianapolis and Broad Ripple, IN focused on bringing the taste of authentic Mexican food to its customers.

OVERVIEW


For this small project I was tasked with creating a simple landing site to display Luciana's most recent food items. I focused on utilizing Bootstrap 5's carousel component, utilizing CSS grid, and focused on responsiveness for mobile.



CHALLENGES


I would say that the most challenging aspect of this project was getting used to using the different properties for the grid. I became accustomed to using it more so after Youtubing a couple of tutorials and further testing it out on CodePen. Another feature I wanted to implement was using bootstraps carousel component which was a pretty straight forward incorporation into the project with a few minor tweeks I had to adjust.


THE SOLUTION


The solution for creating this ad site was to come up with a plan to:


  • 1. Display the new line of watches
  • 2. Give insight into the new line of smart watches
  • 3. Display the information provided in a aesthetically pleasing manner.

While straightforeward and simple, the overall goal of this website landing page was to show the most recent Mexican food offerings in a simplistic and straightforeward manner. I chose to utilize the carousel along with a title and brief description of the food item to do this.
